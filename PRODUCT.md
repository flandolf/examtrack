# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Victorian VCE Units 3–4 students reviewing timed practice exams at home or school.

## Product Purpose

Record exam results, compare them with official VCAA grade distributions where available, and turn mistakes into a practical revision list.

## Brand Personality

Focused, calm, and credible.

## Anti-references

No gamified streak dashboard, decorative education theme, marketing layout, or bespoke component styling.

## Design Principles

- Put the next study action ahead of decorative metrics.
- Keep official data and estimates clearly distinguished.
- Make exam entry fast enough to use immediately after a timed paper.
- Preserve student ownership through local-first storage and portable exports.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA with keyboard navigation, visible focus, reduced-motion support, and chart summaries that do not rely on color.
